This report presents a comprehensive SEO, user experience (UX), and digital growth audit of the MGH Scotland website. The purpose of this analysis is to evaluate the current structure, content strategy, and technical performance of the site, and to identify opportunities for improving its visibility in search engines, user engagement, and lead generation potential.
MGH Scotland operates in the highly specialised field of IT recycling, secure data destruction, and electronic waste management. Businesses operating in this sector depend heavily on digital credibility, regulatory compliance, and operational trust. Organisations seeking recycling partners must be confident that their IT equipment is handled securely, that sensitive data is properly destroyed, and that all activities comply with environmental regulations such as WEEE and AATF licensing standards.
For this reason, a company website in this industry must do more than simply describe services. It must clearly communicate expertise, compliance credentials, operational capability, and service reliability while also providing a seamless pathway for potential clients to request collections or quotations.
The current MGH Scotland website successfully communicates the company’s core services and regulatory compliance, but its structure reflects a traditional informational website rather than a modern search-driven lead generation platform. Opportunities exist to strengthen the site’s information architecture, improve navigation clarity, expand SEO content coverage, and introduce stronger conversion pathways that guide visitors toward enquiries.
This report therefore evaluates the website across several critical dimensions, including:
• Website structure and navigation architecture
 • Homepage messaging and conversion optimisation
 • Service page structure and clarity
 • SEO keyword targeting and content architecture
 • Internal linking strategy and topical authority
 • Trust signals and compliance visibility
 • Technical SEO performance considerations
 • Long-term organic growth opportunities
